About me

I am a Full Stack Kotlin/Java Android Developer based in Seattle currently looking to make the change into tech.

My tech journey started a couple years after graduating from the University of Washington. After losing both my jobs from COVID-19 and unsure what to do with my degree, I rethought about what I wanted to do with my career. The Code Fellows bootcamp presented a great opportunity for me to back to school and learn a new skill. Through the course, I gained a deep appreciation of software development and continued my quest to explore more within Android Development specifically.

I am eager to constantly embrace new Android technologies to make my mobile applications more user friendly. At the moment, my focus has been developing apps in Kotlin which has been a smooth transition from Java since they are similar languages. I also have been doing some research on Machine Learning, Android Jetpack, Flutter, and Google Assistant. Though I am still a novice at best, I made it a goal to continually hone my skills within Android Development.

In my spare time, I "try" to stay fit and active. A few activities I enjoy doing are snowboarding, rock climbing or just heading to the gym. My favorite hobby I appreciate doing is playing video games. I grew up playing all different kinds of gaming systems and recently made the switch to PC after building my computer a few years ago. One of my personal goals is to be a father of a corgi dog. I told myself once I recieve a job offer, I would treat myself in acheiving this goal.
